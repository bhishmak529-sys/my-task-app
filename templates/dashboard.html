Welcome to your Ultimate Productivity Tool! 🚀

TaskPro Elite is an enterprise-grade task management system. This application helps you track your daily tasks, college assignments, and urgent projects. Read the guide below to learn how to utilize the app to its fullest!

📖 How to Use (User Guide)

When you add a new task from the top form, it automatically goes into the Backlog.

👉 Trick: Click and hold any task card to drag and drop it into the To Do or In Progress column. Once completed, drop it into Done and enjoy the celebration 🎉!

Click on any task card! A detailed pop-up will open where you can:
✅ Write a detailed Description for the task.
✅ Create a Checklist (Sub-tasks) for smaller steps.
✅ Upload necessary Images or PDF files, which you can view in full-screen later.

Forgot when you moved your last task?
Click the purple 📜 Activity Log button in the top navigation bar. The app silently saves every action with a date and time stamp, allowing you to view your complete audit trail.

👉 Dashboard: Click the 📊 Dashboard button at the top to view visual graphs tracking your productivity.
👉 Smart Emails: If you add a task under the "High Priority" or "Urgent" category, our background bot will instantly send you an email alert!
